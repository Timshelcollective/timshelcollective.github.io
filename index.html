"Never doubt that a small group of thoughtful, committed, citizens can change the world. Indeed, it is the only thing that ever has"

~ Margaret Mead

We are a digital collective for young adults, especially south Asian women to discuss, learn and exercise their political agency and solidarity actions. We collectivise the individualised self-actualising influence of educated, economically stable youth into a powerful collective force and give them a tool to express themselves better in a medium of their choice.

EDUCATE

Organise learning programmes to understand and learn more about everyday activism, solidarity actions and prevalent human rights movements across the world.

ADVOCATE

Rally together for common feminist goals, curate community resource/support kits and research more ways to strengthen our agency.

MOTIVATE

Provide a platform to create and share our stories and campaign of solidarity and political action.

The Smallest Is The Mightiest

Studies* show that women take more than 70% of consumption-related decisions, be it food, clothing, furniture or cars in families. They tend to be in charge of the health of their families and continue to be a  major influence on both the education and activities of family members. Then why not political decisions?

Society has used a mix of stereotypes, sexism, ageism and patriarchy to keep youth and women away from politics. How often we are asked to -- better not venture or deliberate on politics or talk about it because, in the estimation of most, if not all, "politics is not our cup of tea". Having always been looked down upon and admonished for discussing system change in the drawing-rooms of our house to boardrooms of our organisations, this cynicism and hopelessness to be able to do nothing has set deep in us, enough to make us forget the power we hold in our individual agency. But no more, as individuals we hold the most influential power in our vote, wallet and voice and at Timshel our vision is to help awaken, strengthen and organise this agency so we can shape the change we want to see. As they say, if not us, then who and everything in this galaxy is made up of tiny single units. The smallest is the mightiest.
